Elven Barracks

The Elven Barracks are unlike the grim stone fortresses of men or the crude camps of orcs. They embody discipline and readiness, yet remain in harmony with the elegance of elven architecture. Built from smooth stone and enchanted wood, their walls are etched with glowing runes of protection. Inside the barracks, one can find training grounds surrounded by flowering trees, where archers hone their marksmanship and warriors practice swordplay under the watchful gaze of seasoned captains. Armor racks shimmer with polished silver and green, and banners bearing moonlit emblems sway gently in the breeze. The barracks are not only places of martial discipline but also schools of honor, where young elves are taught that strength must always serve wisdom and harmony.

Erathel – Barracks of the Capital

In Erathel, the City of Wisdom, the barracks stand close to the Great Library and the High Council’s chambers. They are more ceremonial than militaristic, for Erathel’s warriors are guardians of knowledge and sacred places. The barracks here serve as a home for spellblades, mage-knights trained to weave sword and sorcery together. Their duty is not only to defend the city but also to safeguard the ancient tomes and relics stored in Erathel’s heart. Unlike in other cities, Erathel’s barracks also house scholars who study the philosophy of warfare, seeking to ensure that elven blades are always wielded in defense, never conquest.

Levandell – Barracks of the City of Song

In Levandell, the City of Song and Bloom, the barracks appear less like a fortress and more like an open pavilion surrounded by gardens. Training takes place in fields filled with wildflowers, and archery ranges are designed to mimic natural landscapes — forests, rivers, and open meadows — ensuring that the warriors are always attuned to the living world around them. Levandell’s warriors are renowned scouts and healers, trained in both combat and the arts of restoration. Their barracks often echo with music during evenings, as soldiers and artists alike share songs, reminding the elves that beauty and battle can coexist.

Arethon – Barracks of the Sentinel City

In Arethon, the northern sentinel of the kingdom, the barracks are formidable and imposing, carved into the cliffs and reinforced with wards of protection. They are the stronghold of the Sentinels of the Moon, the elite order of rangers and guardians who defend the kingdom’s borders. Here, training is rigorous and relentless: archers drill from high towers, bladesmen spar on stone terraces, and riders prepare for swift movement across mountain passes. Unlike the ceremonial guards of Erathel or the harmonious scouts of Levandell, the warriors of Arethon are hardened defenders, living with one purpose — to ensure that no enemy trespasses into elven lands.
