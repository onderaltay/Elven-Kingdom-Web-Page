Levandell – The City of Song and Bloom

Levandell lies in the southern reaches of the kingdom, where the forest opens into meadows filled with wildflowers and crystal-clear streams. It is known as the City of Song, for its people are artists, musicians, and dreamers. Unlike the lofty heights of Erathel, Levandell’s dwellings are carved into flowering hillsides or built upon floating gardens that drift slowly along the riverbanks. Every evening, music fills the air — elven harps, flutes, and voices blending with the rustling leaves and rushing waters. Festivals of light and bloom are frequent, celebrating not conquest but the turning of seasons, the blossoming of flowers, and the eternal cycle of life. Levandell is the heart of elven culture, where travelers who earn the rare privilege of entering often believe they have stumbled into a dream. Levandell is also the center of healing magic and herbal lore. Its gardens grow rare plants found nowhere else, said to be kissed by moonlight and carrying the essence of the stars. Many wounded warriors, both elven and foreign, have found their lives saved by Levandell’s healers.
