Erathel – The City of Wisdom

Erathel, the spiritual and political capital of the Elven Kingdom, rests within the branches of colossal silverwood trees whose roots reach deep into enchanted soil. Homes and halls are woven seamlessly into the forest canopy, glowing softly with magical lanterns that never fade. The city is famed for its Great Library of Erathel, where tomes bound in living bark record millennia of history, philosophy, and magic. Erathel is a place of scholars, priests, and spellcasters. It is said that every elf who enters the city feels a quiet hum beneath their feet — the pulse of the ancient magic that sustains the kingdom. The High Council gathers here, and foreign envoys, when rarely permitted, find Erathel overwhelming in both majesty and quiet authority. Its tall spires of living wood rise like extensions of the forest itself, making the city nearly invisible to outsiders who might wander too close.
